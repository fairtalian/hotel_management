The Location

Vero Beach boasts 26 miles of pristine shoreline, more parks and preserves per capita than any county in Florida, and an extensive water and land conservation program under the Indian River Land Trust. Vero has been rated one of the safest communities in Florida and was named one of the “100 Best Art Towns in America.” 8050 is perfectly located in between John’s Island and Windsor and is convenient to Vero’s charming village, quaint shops, fabulous restaurants and world-class cultural amenities.
